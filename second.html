Breathing Exercise

Inhale for 4 seconds(1..2..3..4)
Hold your breath for 4 seconds(1..2..3..4)
Exhale for 4 seconds(1..2..3..4)
hold on for 4 seconds(1..2..3..4)

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding technique

  • 5 things you see
  • 4 things you feel
  • 3 things you hear
  • 2 things you smell
  • 1 thing you taste

Visualization

Stay still in your position , slowly relax your shoulder , your eyebrows, your facial muscles.

Take slow breath in through your nose and exhale through your mouth.

slowly close your eyes while still breathing in and out. now start imagining your safe space , it can be a real place or an imaginary.

Slowly slowly notice the small fragments and details on it. Once you ared one creating and visualizing the full image , open your eyes.

Staying present

Find a quiet and comfortable place to sit and close your eyes. Start brething in and out slowly.

Start observing the breathing and body chnages especially around your chest area when you do so.

Concentrate on it till your mind calms down.
