Visualization

Stay still in your position, slowly relax your shoulders, eyebrows, and facial muscles.

Take a slow breath in through your nose and exhale through your mouth.

Slowly close your eyes while still breathing in and out.

Staying Present

Find a quiet and comfortable place to sit and close your eyes.

Start breathing in and out slowly.

Observe the breathing and body changes, especially around your chest area.

Concentrate on it until your mind calms down.
